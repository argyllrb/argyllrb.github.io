Fix by Julie Tsang

Unbroken Theatre

Kevin is a repairman who responds to a call out from an old woman, Li Na, at her house in the woods. Fix is a two hander weaving chinese mythology with a tale of guilt and childhood memory.

The show was performed in a black box studio, in the round. My sound design sought to create the space of the old, remote house in the woods, underscoring and supporting the actors with the shifts of tension and unease within the play.

The excerpt follows a section of text where mythical elements begin to bleed through and unsettle Kevin. Li Na says her cat Zhen Zhen is the cause of the noise upstairs but it’s possible it may be something else entirely.

The QLab outputs have been translated to a binaural mix to better represent the original space
Headphones recommended
